Top Movies by Helene

These are my 3 top movies:

Spirit: Stallion of the Cimarron

My favourite movie as a kid, as I loved both horses and that age in time. It's beautifully made by Dreamworks!

Lord of the Rings (Trilogy)

Fantastic movies based on the books, with amazing cast and writing. Nothing beats this trilogy when you want a movie marathon.

Pirates of the Caribbean

The music grabs me every time, and the acting is superb. Johnny Depp became my favourite actor of all time after watching this.
